Davao City

Davao City is a large, clean, and safe city in Mindanao, known for Mount Apo, durian, the Kadayawan Festival, and being a major economic and cultural hub in the south of the Philippines.

Mount Apo

The highest mountain in the Philippines.

Durian

Iconic fruit, often called "smells like hell, tastes like heaven."

Kadayawan Festival

Colorful August festival celebrating Davao's indigenous roots and harvest.

Personal Enecdotes

During my first visit to Davao, I tried durian at Magsaysay Park. I gagged at first but ended up buying durian ice cream later that night—turns out, it grows on you! Then I watched a tribal dance at the Kadayawan festival—raw, powerful, unforgettable.
